Workflows

Suggestion: Name your workflows using a verb in the imperative, e.g. "archive", "develop".

GOTCHA

When you pull, the workflow configuration is read from the local environment!

The means you MUST always keep .live_dev_porter/config.yml in sync between environments otherwise strange things being to happen.
